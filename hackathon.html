Welcome to JAIPUR

Jaipur is the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan in Northern India. It was founded on 18 November 1727 by Jai Singh II,the ruler of Amer after whom the city is named.

, Jaipur's bazaars sell embroidered leather shoes, blue pottery,Known as the Pink City because of the colour of the stone used exclusively in the walled city tie and dye scarves and other exotic wares. Western Rajasthan itself forms a convenient circuit, in the heart of the Thar desert which has shaped its history, lifestyles and architecture.
